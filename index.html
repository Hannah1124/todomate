Our Story

More than a drink

Todo Mate is about bringing the warmth of South American mate culture to Australia. Mate is not just a drink — it is a shared ritual, a moment to slow down, and a simple way to connect with people around you.

We curate beginner-friendly yerba mate, gourds, bombillas and starter kits for people who want to discover mate in a simple, beautiful and approachable way.
