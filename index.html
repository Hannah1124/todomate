Our Story

Why mate?

Yerba mate is more than a drink. Across South America, it is a daily ritual shared between friends, family and community — one gourd, one bombilla, passed from hand to hand.

It is a simple invitation to slow down, talk honestly and be present with each other. More than a beverage, mate carries a culture of sharing, warmth and connection.

ToDo Mate brings this ritual to Australia for people who are curious about a different way of gathering: slower, warmer and more meaningful.
